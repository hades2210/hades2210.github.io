Main activities

Research and Development in Electron Microscopy

Our association is dedicated to advancing the field of electron microscopy through cutting-edge research and development. We collaborate with academic and industry partners to explore new techniques and applications.

Learn More
Educational Workshops and Seminars

Join us to learn more about the intricacies of electron microscopy. We host workshops and seminars led by experts in the field, catering to both beginners and advanced users

Learn More
Microscope Equipment Loans and Grants

We provide access to state-of-the-art electron microscopes for researchers and institutions that might not have the resources to own such equipment. Explore our equipment loan programs and research grants.

Learn More
Publication and Resource Sharing

Stay informed about the latest developments in electron microscopy. Our association publishes a range of materials, including research papers, technical guides, and case studies.
